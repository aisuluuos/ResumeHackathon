Projects & Achievements

Leadership is a vital skill that is essential for success in any school
or community, and I am proud to say that I have had ample opportunity
to develop and apply my leadership skills in various settings. My
experiences as a president of the student council, co-founder of the
nonprofit organization "Unity," finalist in the Technovation Girls
Kyrgyzstan project under the US Embassy and participating competitions and
Olympiads in the Computer Science field have helped me to hone my
communication, problem-solving, and decision-making skills, which are
essential for effective leadership. For a long time, my life consisted
of training in taekwondo and my project to open the first volunteer
organization in our school. These are the very things that I am most
proud of. Sports have always been an integral part of my life and I was
finally able to achieve my black belt, I am grateful to myself for the
persistence and hard work and also for not giving up after the injury and
finally achieving my goal. Throughout high school, we were able to start
our club and completed a lot of cool projects, which I'm very proud of.
One of the main projects that I led was a project to help children from
low-income families and orphans obtain soft skills and assistance (tutoring)
in subjects such as mathematics, English and Russian.
